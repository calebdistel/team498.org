The Cobra Commanders

About Us

Team 498 | The Cobra Commanders was established in the Fall of 2000 by Rob Mainieri, a former Math teacher at Cactus. Today the team is led by Lead Mentor, Adam Shroyer. Adam is a former founding student of Team 498 and current teacher at Cactus High School. Over the team’s 22-year history it has grown from one of the smallest programs in Arizona to one of the state’s longest tenured teams.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and scientists by providing a fun and educational environment for students to learn and grow. We strive to create a team that is inclusive and welcoming to all students, regardless of their background or experience. We hope to instill a passion for STEM in our students and to help them develop the skills necessary to succeed in the future.
